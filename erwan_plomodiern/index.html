Plomodiern, Finistere, France

Plomodiern's history

Plomodieren's name is present in some historic accounts from the IXth century. The name means parish of the Saint Modiern. The actual church was built in the XVIIth century.

Where to find and how to go

You can go from Paris to Quimper by train and then With bus from Quimper to Plomodiern. Pay attention to the fact there aren't many buses each day. Here the link to the bus service.

In Brittany the Fauna is pretty rich on or under the rocks. One good idea is to go on the rocky parts and do coastal foraging, to find food or just discover the fascinating life hidden under the algae.

A little city that kept its medieval flair. Definitely worth a visit! More informations.

Due to the geography of the coast and the lights of the town, many fish species come to the shore to feed and many people come too to catch them: mackerels, jack mackerels, garfishes, european basses, skids, ...

Quimper is the main city in the south of Finistere. You would see there old houses with half-timbering and the St Corentin Cathedral. More informations about Quimper here.

In Brittany tradition and culture is still strong. That's why it's easy to find "Fest noz" (evening dancing) every week-end where young and less young people dance on traditional music. You'll find info about this events on websites, newspaper or asking the tourism office about it. In the same genre there are many festivals where you'll see people wearing traditional clothes, playing music and dancing. In plomodiern you have the festival du Menez-Hom around mid august but you'll find others in the region, mostly in summer.

Plomodiern has sevral beaches on its coast and if you want to wander farther, the choice will be even greater of course.

Among the sea activities, you can try out kayak with the club nautique de plomodiern situated in front of the beach Porz ar Vag. You may find other possibilities next to other beaches of the region as well.

When the waves are coming, you can also surf. The most famous spot in the region is the pointe de la torche around 50 kilometers away south.

When the tide is low, you can practice sand yachting on the beach "pentrez" on the Saint-Nic municipality. You'll find the most usefull informations following this link.
