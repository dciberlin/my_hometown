Plomodiern, Finistere, France

Plomodiern's history

Plomodieren's name is present in some historic accounts from the IXth century. The name means parish of the Saint Modiern.

Where to find and how to go

You can go from Paris to Quimper by train and then With bus from Quimper to Plomodiern. Pay attention to the fact there aren't many buses each day. Here the link to the bus service.

Examples of activities in plomodiern and the region

Shore fishing

In Brittany the Fauna is pretty rich on or under the rocks. One good idea is to go on the rocky parts and do coastal foraging, to find food or just discover the fascinating life hidden under the algae.

Locronan

A little city that kept its medieval flair. Definitely worth a visit! More informations

Fishing in the port of Douarnenez

Due to the geography of the coast and the lights of the town, many fish species come to the shore to feed and many people come too to catch them: mackerels, jack mackerels, garfishes, european basses, skids, ...

Visit Quimper

Quimper is the main city in the south of Finistere. You would see there old houses with half-timbering and the St Corentin Cathedral. More informations about Quimper here.
